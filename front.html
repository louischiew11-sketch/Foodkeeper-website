Pro Storage Guidelines

1

First In, First Out (FIFO)

Always cycle older ingredients to the front lines of your shelves so they are reached for first during fast-paced cooking sessions.

2

Ethylene Isolation Rules

Keep items like bananas, apples, and tomatoes separated from green leafy vegetables, as their natural ripening gases accelerate spoilage.

3

Utilize the Freezer Safeguard

If you recognize that a meat profile or a batch of bread won't be used within the week, transfer them into the Freezer node immediately to lock their timeline down.
