What is Food Keeper?

Food Keeper is a web application designed to help you manage your food inventory at home. Add items, track expiry dates, record waste, and make smarter choices for your everyday meals.

Key Features
🧊
Manage Ingredients Store and organise ingredients in your fridge, freezer, and pantry with ease.
🔔
Expiry Alerts Get notified when food is about to expire or already expired.
📊
Waste Tracking Record and monitor food waste by month and over time.
🛒
Smart Shopping Plan your shopping and create smart shopping lists.
🌱
Live More Sustainably Reduce food waste, save money, and help protect the environment.
